My Story

From Farm Fields to Digital Solutions

Growing up in Punjab, Pakistan, I spent my childhood working on our family farm, tending to buffaloes, operating machinery, and understanding the challenges of traditional agriculture. These experiences gave me firsthand knowledge of the inefficiencies in farming practices that technology could solve.

My passion for technology led me to pursue a Bachelor's in Software Engineering, where I specialized in AI and Data Science. Now, I'm combining these two worlds - applying my technical skills to revolutionize agriculture through digital farming innovations.

My final year project, a Sign Language Recognition App using computer vision, demonstrated my ability to create practical AI solutions - a skill I plan to transfer to agricultural applications like crop monitoring and precision farming.
